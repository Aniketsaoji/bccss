Yelp Tech Talk

Interplanetarization: Launching Yelp in the Klingon Empire

  • Date: Febuary 4th, 2014

Yelp will be checking in at Boston College as a part of our whirlwind Boston area Tech Talk Tour! You’re invited to come and to learn more about Yelp tech and culture from one of our very own! Join Alex L. as he explores an exciting new frontier for Yelp--an interplanetary launch! Alex will dive into deep space and explore the possibility of expanding Yelp’s presence beyond earth. In his talk, he will focus on the processes and potential challenges of deploying Yelp on a new planet. He will also discuss how our Contextual Translations tool would allow our in-house “extraterrestrial linguistics experts” to preview their translations in real-time.
